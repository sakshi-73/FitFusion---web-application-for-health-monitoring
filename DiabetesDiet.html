Here are a few easy and Diabetes-friendly Recipes:

Grilled Chicken Salad:

  • Ingredients:
  • Skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • Mixed salad greens (spinach, arugula, or lettuce)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil and balsamic vinegar for dressing
  • Instructions:
  • Season chicken with herbs, grill until fully cooked.
  • Slice grilled chicken into strips.
  • Toss salad greens,cherry tomatoes,cucumber in a bowl.
  • Top the salad with grilled chicken strips.
  • Drizzle with a mixture of olive oil, balsamic vinegar.
