Exercises for underweight person

1.Bodyweight Squats

  • Stand with feet hip-width apart.
  • Lower your body as if sitting back into a chair.
  • Keep your chest up and knees behind toes.
  • Push through your heels to return to standing.

    • 2.Push-Ups

  • Start in a plank position with hands shoulder-width apart.
  • Lower your body until your chest almost touches the floor.
  • Push back up to the starting position.

    • 3.Dumbbell Rows

  • Hold a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing each other.
  • Bend your knees slightly and hinge forward at the hips.
  • Pull the dumbbells up towards your ribs, squeezing your shoulder blades.

    • 4.Lunges

  • Stand with feet hip-width apart.
  • Take a step forward with one foot and lower your body until both knees are bent at 90 degrees.
  • Push back to the starting position and switch legs
