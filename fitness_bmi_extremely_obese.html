Exercises for extremely obese person

1.Wall Push-Ups

  • Stand facing a wall at arm's length.
  • Lean forward and push against the wall, then push back to starting position.

    • 2.Water Walking

  • Walk in a shallow pool or use a flotation device for support.
  • Move your arms and legs in a walking motion.

    • 3.Recumbent Bike

  • Provides back support and is gentle on joints.
  • Start with a comfortable resistance and pedal at a moderate pace.

    • 4.Seated Cardio Exercises

  • Perform seated leg cycling or arm cycling with a resistance band.
  • Focus on continuous movement to elevate heart rate.
