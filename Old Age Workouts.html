Walking :-

  • Low-impact and a great cardiovascular exercise.
  • Can be done outdoors or on a treadmill.

Water Aerobics :-

  • Gentle on the joints while providing resistance.
  • Improves cardiovascular fitness.

Cycling:-

  • Stationary or outdoor biking for leg strength.
  • Low impact on joints.

Chair Exercises :-

  • Seated leg lifts, seated marches, and seated twists.
  • Suitable for those with limited mobility.

Resistance Band Workout :-

  • Gentle strength training for various muscle groups.
  • Improves muscle tone.
