Exercises for obese person

1.Chair Squats

  • Sit on a chair with feet flat on the floor.
  • Stand up without using your hands, then sit back down.

    • 2.Seated Leg Press

  • Use a leg press resistance bands.
  • Sit comfortably and press your legs forward against resistance.

    • 3.Upper Body Exercises

  • Perform seated or standing bicep curls, shoulder presses, and tricep extensions with light weights or resistance bands.

    • 4.Water Aerobics

  • Low-impact exercises in water.
  • Include movements like water walking, leg lifts, and arm circles.
