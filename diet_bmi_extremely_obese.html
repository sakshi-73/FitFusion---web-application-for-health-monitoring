7 - Day Diet Plan For Extremely Obese Person

Day 1

  • Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM) : Idli (brown rice), sambhar/ 2 whole eggs + toast
  • Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM) : 1 cup papaya
  • Lunch (2:00-2:30PM) : Whole grain roti + mixed vegetable / chicken
  • Evening (4:00-4:30PM) : 5 almonds + unsweetened tea or coffee
  • Dinner (8:00-8:30PM) : Soup with sauteed veg

Day 2

  • Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM) : Atta or moong dal chilla / scrambled eggs + toast
  • Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM) : 1 cup coconut water
  • Lunch (2:00-2:30PM) : Brown rice + gravy sabzi or dal
  • Evening (4:00-4:30PM) : Fruits + unsweetened tea or coffee
  • Dinner (8:00-8:30PM) : Porridge with salad

Day 3

  • Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM) : Porridge / fruits with milk / omelette
  • Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM) : 1 apple
  • Lunch (2:00-2:30PM) : Whole grain roti + paneer / vegetable salad
  • Evening (4:00-4:30PM) : 2 dates + unsweetened tea or coffee
  • Dinner (8:00-8:30PM) : Brown rice + paneer or steamed fish

Day 4

  • Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM) : Oats and yogurt/vegetable omelet
  • Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM) : 1 cup musk melon
  • Lunch (2:00-2:30PM) : Whole grain roti + vegetables / sauteed chicken roti wrap
  • Evening (4:00-4:30PM) : 5 almonds + 5 walnuts + buttermilk
  • Dinner (8:00-8:30PM) : Brown rice + masala chana/chicken

Day 5

  • Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM) : Daliya / French toast
  • Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM) : 1 cup buttermilk
  • Lunch (2:00-2:30PM) : Sambar and rice
  • Evening (4:00-4:30PM) : 5 almonds + 2 dates + unsweetened tea or coffee
  • Dinner (8:00-8:30PM) : Vegetable pulao / chicken pulao + curd

Day 6

  • Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM) : Parathas / egg + roti
  • Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM) : 1 cup watermelon
  • Lunch (2:00-2:30PM) : Whole grain roti + gravy sabzi/chicken curry with quinoa
  • Evening (4:00-4:30PM) : Fruits + unsweetened tea or coffee
  • Dinner (8:00-8:30PM) : Dal ka chilla with grilled paneer or fish

Day 7

  • Breakfast (8:00-8:30AM) : Oats with milk and fruits
  • Mid-Meal (11:00-11:30AM) : 1 cup coconut water
  • Lunch (2:00-2:30PM) : Whole grain roti + sabzi / soup + salad
  • Evening (4:00-4:30PM) : Yogurt with fruits
  • Dinner (8:00-8:30PM) : Tandoori paneer / chicken/ fish
